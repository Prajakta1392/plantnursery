Reviews:-

"Fantastic Nursery"

This is THE place in the area to find all your gardening and landscaping needs. There are so many choices and wonderful staff to help you out if you have a question. Prices are reasonable and plenty of parking. There is also a special activity, the Butterfly Encounter, there to visit when you have a little extra time. They also have a reward program and during your birth month you get $10 off your purchase.

"Impressive Nursery"

This is a large nursery, with an impressive assortment of plants and trees, both fruit bearing and non-fruit bearing. The scenery of the layout is a beautiful sight, worth visiting even if not shopping for anything at this nursery. Of course, we were happy with selection offered and ended up purchasing a fruit tree.
